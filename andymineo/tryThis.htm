Who Is Andy Mineo
Andy Mineo: born April 2, 1987; Atlanta, Georgia; released mixtape Heroes for Sale in 2009; first signed with Reach Records; latest record Uncomfortable released in 2015; founded 116 Clique in 2010 with Sho Baraka, Lecrae, Derek Minor, Canon, Tedashii; currently on Winter Jam US tour
